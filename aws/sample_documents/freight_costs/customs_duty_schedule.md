### USMCA (Mexico/Canada) Preferential Rates:
- Steel from Mexico (Monterrey Steel Works): 0% duty, 0% Section 232 surcharge
- Canadian composites (Canadian Lumber & Composites): 0% duty
- Automotive parts from Mexico (Guadalajara): 0% if meeting rules of origin

### Important Notes:
1. Section 301 tariffs on Chinese goods: Additional 25% on most manufactured goods from China
2. Anti-dumping duties may apply to specific products from certain countries
3. Goods must meet rules of origin requirements for preferential treatment
4. Free Trade Zone (FTZ) benefits available for manufacturing operations
5. Temporary importation bond available for goods re-exported within 1 year
6. Duty drawback available for re-exported manufactured goods (99% recovery)
