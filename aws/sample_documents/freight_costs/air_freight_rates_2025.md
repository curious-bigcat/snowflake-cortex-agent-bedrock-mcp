### Surcharges and Fees:
- Dangerous goods handling: +$2.50/kg (Class 3, 8, 9 accepted)
- Oversized cargo (>300cm any dimension): +30% on base rate
- Weekend/holiday pickup: +$250 flat fee
- Customs brokerage: $150 per shipment (import), $85 per shipment (export)
- Lithium battery surcharge (Section II): +$1.80/kg
- Temperature-controlled cargo: +$3.00/kg
- Insurance: 0.35% of declared value (minimum $50)

### Volume Discounts:
- 500-1000 kg/month: 5% discount
- 1000-5000 kg/month: 10% discount
- 5000+ kg/month: 15% discount + dedicated account manager

### Transit Time Notes:
- Shanghai/Shenzhen routes may experience 1-2 day delays during Chinese New Year and Golden Week
- Customs clearance typically adds 0.5-1 day at destination
- Stuttgart route via Frankfurt hub with same-day connection
