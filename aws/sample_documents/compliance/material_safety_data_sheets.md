# Material Safety Data Sheet (Summary)
## Chemicals Used in Manufacturing Operations

### 1. Industrial Epoxy Resin (EC-5200)
- **Supplier**: EuroChemicals AG
- **CAS Number**: 25068-38-6
- **Hazard Classification**: GHS07 (Irritant), GHS09 (Environmental)
- **Signal Word**: WARNING
- **PPE Required**: Chemical-resistant gloves, safety goggles, ventilated workspace
- **Storage**: Cool, dry place below 25°C. Shelf life 365 days.
- **Spill Response**: Absorb with inert material (vermiculite). Dispose as hazardous waste.
- **Fire Fighting**: CO2, dry chemical, foam. Do not use water spray.

### 2. Hydraulic Fluid (HF-320)
- **Supplier**: Finnish Specialty Chemicals
- **CAS Number**: 68476-85-7
- **Hazard Classification**: GHS02 (Flammable), GHS07 (Irritant)
- **Signal Word**: DANGER
- **PPE Required**: Nitrile gloves, safety glasses, coveralls
- **Storage**: Below 40°C, away from ignition sources. Shelf life 545 days.
- **Note**: PFAS-free formulation effective June 2025 (REACH compliant)
- **Environmental**: Do not release to waterways. Biodegradable within 28 days.

### 3. Thermal Paste (EC-7700 Series - DISCONTINUED Sep 2025)
- **Supplier**: EuroChemicals AG
- **CAS Number**: 63148-62-9 (base)
- **Hazard Classification**: GHS07 (Irritant)
- **Signal Word**: WARNING
- **PPE Required**: Latex gloves
- **Storage**: Below 25°C. Shelf life 180 days.
- **IMPORTANT**: Contains SVHC substance. Replacement: EC-8800 series (12.5 W/mK, fully REACH compliant)
- **Last Order Date**: July 15, 2025

### 4. Anodizing Chemical Bath (ACB-450)
- **Supplier**: EuroChemicals AG
- **CAS Number**: Multiple (sulfuric acid, chromic acid blend)
- **Hazard Classification**: GHS05 (Corrosive), GHS06 (Toxic), GHS09 (Environmental)
- **Signal Word**: DANGER
- **PPE Required**: Full face shield, acid-resistant apron, chemical gloves, respiratory protection
- **Storage**: Dedicated chemical storage room. Shelf life 180 days.
- **Disposal**: Licensed hazardous waste contractor only
- **Regulatory**: Subject to chromium VI restrictions under REACH

### 5. Conformal Coating (CC-500)
- **Supplier**: Finnish Specialty Chemicals
- **CAS Number**: 9003-36-5 (acrylic base)
- **Hazard Classification**: GHS02 (Flammable), GHS07 (Irritant)
- **Signal Word**: WARNING
- **PPE Required**: Solvent-resistant gloves, safety goggles, ventilation
- **Storage**: Below 30°C, away from heat/flame. Shelf life 180 days.
- **Application**: Spray or dip. Allow 2-hour cure at room temperature or 30 min at 80°C.

### 6. Solder Paste (SAC305)
- **Supplier**: Multiple (per plant)
- **Composition**: Sn96.5/Ag3.0/Cu0.5
- **Hazard Classification**: GHS07 (Irritant)
- **Signal Word**: WARNING
- **PPE Required**: ESD gloves, safety glasses
- **Storage**: Refrigerated 2-10°C. Room temperature working life: 8 hours max.
- **CRITICAL**: Expired flux activator caused solder defect at Shenzhen plant (March 2025).
  Implement FIFO and automated lot expiry tracking.
